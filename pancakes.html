Pancake

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 cups flour
  • 3 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1 cups milk

DIRECTIONS

  1. Mix the dry items first.
  2. Combine the eggs and melted butter to the milk and slowly stir into the flour mixture.
  3. Let sit at least 10 minutes while heating the pan. These will be high, but light and fluffy.
